Thyme

Thymus

-Care Guide-

Sun:
Full Indirect Sun 6-8hrs/day

Soil/Water:
Be sure to plant in pot with drainage holes. Drought resistant and prefers light, alkaline soil on the drier side. Do not over-water.

Harvesting:
Pick small amounts all year especially in the summer when thyme is in bloom. Cut no more than a third of the plant at any given time.
