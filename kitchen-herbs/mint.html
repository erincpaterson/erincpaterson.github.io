Mint

Mentha

-Care Guide-

Sun:
Full to Partial Indirect Sun 6-8hrs/day

Soil/Water:
Be sure to plant in pot with drainage holes. Soil should be kept somewhat moist. Prefers well-drained alkaline soil, rich in nutrients.

Harvesting:
Pick small amounts all year. Gather main leaf crop just before flowering. Cut no more than a third of the plant at any given time.
