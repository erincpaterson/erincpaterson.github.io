Rosemary

Rosmarinus officinalis

-Care Guide-

Sun:
Full Indirect Sun 6-8hrs/day Protect from cold winds.

Soil/Water:
Be sure to plant in pot with drainage holes. Prefers sandy or limy soil. Mixing with eggshells, or potash provides additional lime. Do not over-water.

Harvesting:
Pick small amounts all year. Gather just before flowering. Cut no more than a third of the plant at any given time.
