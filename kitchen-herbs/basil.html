Basil

Ocimum basilicum

-Care Guide-

Sun:
Full Indirect Sun 6-8hrs/day Protect from wind, frost, and heat

Soil/Water:
Be sure to plant in pot with drainage holes. Soil should be kept somewhat moist. Use all purpose fertilizer if leaves become pale.

Harvesting:
Basil can reach a height of three feet. Prune every 2 weeks. Pick small amounts all year. Cut no more than a third of the plant at any given time.
